Sequences (11):
  Ģ USER_SEQ through CANCEL_SEQ

  Ģ Hall ? Theatre (cascade delete)

  Ģ Booking ? Shows, Users, Payment
  Ģ Ticket ? Booking, Seat, Cancellation, Price

Indexes:
  Ģ Movie (ReleaseDate)
